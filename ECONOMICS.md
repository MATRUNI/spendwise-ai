# Unit Economics

## Value of a Converted Lead to Credex
If Credex deploys this tool, the ultimate goal is not just to sell a consultation, but to convert the lead into a long-term user of the core Credex platform (corporate cards/spend management). 
- **Assumption:** A Series A company spends $500k/year on general SaaS and T&E.
- **Revenue:** If Credex earns a 1.5% interchange fee on that spend, that customer generates **$7,500/year** in pure interchange revenue.
- **LTV:** Assuming a 4-year lifespan before an exit/failure, the Lifetime Value (LTV) of a converted lead is roughly **$30,000**.

## CAC Breakdown by Channel
From our GTM plan, we have two primary channels:
1. **Cold Email Outreach:** 
   - Cost: $100/mo for Apollo.io + $30/mo for an email sequencer = $130 fixed cost.
   - Yield: 300 emails sent → 10% click rate (30 audits) → 5% book consultation (1.5 leads).
   - CAC: $130 / 1.5 = **~$86 per booked consultation**.
2. **Community Tear-Downs (Reddit/Slack):**
   - Cost: $0 (Founder time).
   - Yield: 50 audits → 10% book consultation (5 leads).
   - CAC: **$0**.

Blended CAC for a booked consultation is incredibly low (under $50) because the tool itself acts as the lead magnet.

## The Conversion Math to Profitability
To ensure this specific lead-gen tool is highly profitable for Credex, we need to track the funnel from "Audit" to "Swiping a Credex Card".
- **Target Funnel:**
  1. 100 Audits Completed
  2. 10% trigger the >$10k savings CTA = 10 High-Intent Users
  3. 20% of High-Intent Users book the consultation = 2 Booked Calls
  4. 50% of Calls convert to a Credex credit purchase/platform signup = 1 New Customer.
- **Conclusion:** We need a **1% overall conversion rate** (from Audit → Customer). Given a $30,000 LTV and a sub-$50 lead CAC, the LTV:CAC ratio is astronomically high, making the tool instantly profitable.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To drive $1,000,000 in ARR solely from this lead-gen tool, we need to acquire 133 new customers (at $7,500/yr interchange revenue each).
Working backward through our 1% funnel:
- 133 Customers required.
- **13,300 total audits** need to be completed over 18 months.
- That is **~738 audits per month** or ~24 per day.

**What must be true for this to happen:**
1. The AI landscape must remain fragmented and confusing, ensuring companies continue to overspend on redundant tools.
2. The "Report Share" viral loop (Finance sharing the UUID link with Engineering) must maintain a coefficient > 1.0 to drive organic, zero-CAC traffic to hit the 24/day volume target.
